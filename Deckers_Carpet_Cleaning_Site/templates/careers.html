Job Description

Decker's Carpet Cleaning is currently accepting resumes for full and part time Carpet Cleaning Technicians.

NO CARPET CLEANING EXPERIENCE NECESSARY

Who We Are:

Decker's Carpet Cleaning, established in 2011, is a local family run business based in Kingwood, TX. Our mission is to make people happy and improve the environment around us. We do this by keeping their floors and furniture clean in their homes and offices in a friendly, professional, and efficient manner. We feel that our customers should be delighted by our service and amazed by our results.

What We Do:

We visit customers' homes, workplaces, schools and churches where we greet the customer, evaluate their cleaning needs, offer our best solution to their needs with a clearly defined scope and price, complete the service, collect the balance due, enter payment and send receipt. Our goal at each visit is to build a long term relationship with that client.

Our Services Include:

  • Carpet Cleaning using our own proprietary methods
  • Carpet Repair and Stretching
  • Tile and Grout Cleaning
  • Area Rug Cleaning
  • Hard Floor cleaning
  • VCT Strip and Wax
  • Stone Floor Cleaning, Honing and Polishing
  • Upholstery Cleaning
  • Mattress Cleaning
  • Shower Stall Cleaning
  • Auto Interior Cleaning
  • Granite Countertop Cleaning and Polishing
  • Marble Countertop Cleaning and Polishing
  • Emergency Water Extraction and Structural Drying
  • Other Services as they develop

Who You Are:

You have DEMONSTRATED Customer Service skills in a hands on environment (Restaurant Server, Tire Shop, Oil Change shop, Retail, or in home service provider or similar position)

You're passionate about SOMETHING!

You realize this is a job where you will be on your toes with customers, on your feet cleaning, and you're here to delight and amaze our clients. That takes enthusiasm.

You're willing to take initiative and learn new things.

Education is important to you and you know it comes in many different forms. You seek it out for your own personal betterment.

You have integrity

You would never dream of cutting corners or being dishonest with customers. You're working to make money, but you must feel you're contributing to something bigger.

You're a good communicator.

You can get to the point while being clear and effective, and you actively listen when people speak. You can read, write, and speak English and maybe even other languages.

You can measure and calculate the area of a room

Building invoices with multiple service items and taxes is a large part of working in the field.

You're in good physical shape.

You can lift 50 plus pounds and work on your feet in various conditions. You're not allergic to cats, dogs, dust, springtime, hard work, or mornings. You can see the full color spectrum to identify spots on the carpet.

You can follow and give direction.

You have a good attitude towards life and take a positive approach to problems.

You pay attention to the details.

You know that the little things matter and that's what sets you apart from your peers.

You can drive a van (or are confident you can learn).

You can navigate traffic, understand online maps, and understand that your van is a giant billboard.


The ideal candidate excels at listening to the customers' needs, finding the best solution, communicating the value of the solution, and delivering a high quality result.
