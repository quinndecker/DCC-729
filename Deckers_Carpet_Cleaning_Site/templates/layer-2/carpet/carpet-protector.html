Why Do You Need Carpet Protectors like Green Guard?

Most people don't believe in buying carpet protectors as they feel it is an unnecessary expense. There's nothing wrong with that, except for one small issue; when you leave your freshly cleaned carpet unprotected, it's more vulnerable to spills, dirt, accidents, and pet issues. An unprotected carpet will absorb all the spills and dirt directly into the fibers.

Over the course of time between cleanings, the dirt and soils that accumulate can actually cause abrasion and distortion of the carpet fibers. This is generally the cause of dark traffic lanes in your most heavily walked on areas, as the pressure from walking grinding against the dirt and soils in the carpet cause fiber distortion. Carpet protectors will form a protective layer around the carpet fibers, leaving them resistant to physical damage such as abrasion and distortion, helping you keep your carpets looking fresh for years.
