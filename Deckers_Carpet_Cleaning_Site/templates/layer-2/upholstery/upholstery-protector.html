Why Do You Need Upholstery Protectors like Green Guard?

Most people don't believe in buying upholstery protectors as they feel it is an unnecessary expense. There's nothing wrong with that, except for one small issue; when you leave your freshly cleaned sofa, chair, sectional, or loveseat unprotected, it's more vulnerable to spills, dirt, accidents, and pet issues. An unprotected upholstered piece of furniture will absorb all the spills and dirt directly into the fibers.

Over the course of time between cleanings, the dirt and soils that accumulate can actually cause abrasion and distortion of the fibers. This is generally the cause of dark spots on the head, arms or seat of your favorite spots on the sofa, or in that comfy chair. Upholstery protectors will form a protective layer around the fibers, leaving them resistant to physical damage such as abrasion and distortion, helping you keep your upholstery looking fresh for years.
