A room is an area up to 200 sqare feet. Generally, larger areas such as Master Bedrooms, Family Rooms, Game Rooms count as 1.5 rooms while smaller areas such as Secondary Bedrooms, Dining Rooms, Offices count as 1 room. Staircases generally count as 1 room. Closets are measured and added to the size of the room and only incur an additional charge if it pushes the room into the next pricing bracket.

A further breakdown of our room sizing is as follows:
Under 100 Sq Ft - . 5 room
100-200 Sq Ft - 1 room
200-300 Sq Ft - 1 . 5 rooms
300-400 Sq Ft - 2 rooms
